## Guidelines for Brainstorming

**DO:**
- Include people who work directly with the process
- Accept all ideas during brainstorming (don't judge)
- Look for evidence to support/refute each cause
- Consider interactions between categories
- Ask "Why?" for each potential cause (feed into 5 Whys)

**DON'T:**
- Blame individuals - look for system issues
- Stop at the first plausible cause
- Assume without verification
- Ignore less obvious categories
