## NOTES FOR USE:

- SOPs are higher-level than Work Instructions
- Focus on "what" and "who", less detail on "how"
- Include process flow diagram for clarity
- Define decision points and escalation paths
- Ensure alignment with quality system requirements
- Link to supporting work instructions
- Update when process changes occur
- Maintain training records for all users
