# Automotive Manufacturing Templates

This directory contains standard templates for automotive manufacturing documentation.

## Available Templates

### 1. Work Instruction Template
**File**: `work-instruction-template.md`
**Use for**: Detailed operator-level step-by-step instructions
**Level**: Task-specific, highly detailed
**Audience**: Production operators

### 2. Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) Template
**File**: `sop-template.md`
**Use for**: Department or process-level procedures
**Level**: Process overview with moderate detail
**Audience**: Supervisors, technicians, cross-functional teams

### 3. Business Process Template
**File**: `business-process-template.md` (if needed)
**Use for**: High-level organizational processes
**Level**: Strategic, minimal detail
**Audience**: Management, process owners

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Copy the template** to your working directory
2. **Rename** with your document number and title
3. **Fill in all sections** - delete sections that don't apply
4. **Add visuals** - photos, diagrams, flowcharts
5. **Review** with subject matter experts
6. **Approve** per your document control procedure
7. **Train** all affected personnel
8. **Implement** and monitor effectiveness

## Template Customization

These templates follow automotive industry best practices but can be customized for your organization:

- Modify header/footer to match your company format
- Adjust approval workflow to match your quality system
- Add/remove sections based on your needs
- Incorporate company branding
- Add customer-specific requirements

## Document Numbering

Recommended numbering scheme:
- **Work Instructions**: `WI-[AREA]-[PROCESS]-[###]`
- **SOPs**: `SOP-[AREA]-[PROCESS]-[###]`
- **Business Processes**: `BP-[AREA]-[PROCESS]-[###]`

Examples:
- `WI-ASM-ENGINE-001` - Assembly, Engine, Work Instruction 001
- `SOP-QUA-INCOMING-005` - Quality, Incoming Inspection, SOP 005
