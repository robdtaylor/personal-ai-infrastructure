# Adjacent Products Reference

Planning frameworks for restomods, continuation vehicles, bespoke commissions, and motorsport programmes.

## 1. Restomods & Continuation Vehicles

### Definitions

**Continuation Vehicle**: Factory-authorised reproduction of a heritage model, typically:
- Built to original specification (or close)
- Limited run (often completing original planned production)
- Uses original tooling where available
- Carries heritage brand cachet

**Restomod**: Classic vehicle body with modern mechanicals:
- Original or reproduction body
- Modern chassis, suspension, brakes
- Contemporary powertrain
- Updated interior/electronics

## 3. Motorsport Programmes

### Programme Types

```
MOTORSPORT PROGRAMME CATEGORIES

FACTORY RACING
├─ Purpose: Works team competing for championships
├─ Investment: £______ - £______ /year
├─ Return: Brand exposure, technology development
└─ Control: Full factory management

CUSTOMER RACING
├─ Purpose: Supply racing cars to private teams
├─ Investment: Car development + support infrastructure
├─ Return: Vehicle sales, parts revenue, brand presence
└─ Control: Product supply, technical support

HOMOLOGATION PROGRAMME
├─ Purpose: Build road cars to enable racing eligibility
├─ Investment: Road car programme (may be loss-leader)
├─ Return: Racing car sales, championship participation
└─ Control: Specification driven by regulations

ONE-MAKE SERIES
├─ Purpose: Organised championship using your vehicles
├─ Investment: Series management + vehicle supply
├─ Return: Vehicle sales, series revenue, brand building
└─ Control: Full series ownership
```
