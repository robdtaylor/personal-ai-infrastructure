### Decision Gate Framework

Each major programme should have defined gates:

**Gate 0: Strategic Intent**
- Market opportunity validated
- Preliminary business case
- Resource availability confirmed
- Decision: Proceed to concept development?

**Gate 1: Concept Approval**
- Concept design complete
- Engineering feasibility confirmed
- Detailed business case
- Decision: Proceed to detailed design?

**Gate 2: Design Freeze**
- Design specification locked
- Supplier nominations complete
- Updated cost estimates
- Decision: Proceed to tooling?

**Gate 3: Tooling Commitment**
- Final production design
- Tooling contracts signed
- Certification plan confirmed
- Decision: Commit full investment?

**Gate 4: Launch Readiness**
- Production validation complete
- Quality targets achieved
- Launch materials ready
- Decision: Approve production release?
