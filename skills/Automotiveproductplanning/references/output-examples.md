## Risk Register Format

Key risks with probability, impact, and mitigations:

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PRODUCT PLAN RISK REGISTER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

RISK SCORING
Probability: 1 (Remote) → 5 (Almost Certain)
Impact: 1 (Negligible) → 5 (Catastrophic)
Score: Probability × Impact

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ID   Risk                           P   I   Score   Owner
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

REGULATORY RISKS
R01  Euro 7 timeline acceleration   3   5   15      [Name]
     Trigger: EU confirms earlier implementation
     Impact: [Model X] may miss compliance deadline
     Mitigation: Accelerate powertrain development, evaluate 
                 hybrid bridge solution
     Contingency: Delay launch, accept limited EU sales initially

R02  UK/EU regulatory divergence    2   3   6       [Name]
     Trigger: UK adopts different standards
     Impact: Dual certification costs, potential market exit
     Mitigation: Monitor closely, maintain flexible architecture
     Contingency: Prioritise markets based on volume/margin

MARKET RISKS
R03  Economic downturn              3   4   12      [Name]
     Trigger: Recession in key markets
     Impact: Volume below forecast, cash flow strain
     Mitigation: Stress-test plan at -20% volume
     Contingency: Defer non-critical programmes, reduce variants

R04  Competitive disruption         2   4   8       [Name]
     Trigger: New entrant or competitor reposition
     Impact: Lost market share, pricing pressure
     Mitigation: Monitor competitive intelligence, preserve 
                 differentiation
     Contingency: Accelerate response programme

TECHNOLOGY RISKS
R05  Battery supply constraint      3   4   12      [Name]
     Trigger: Supplier cannot meet volume/timing
     Impact: BEV programme delayed
     Mitigation: Dual-source strategy, advance ordering
     Contingency: Extend hybrid production, delay BEV SOP

R06  Technology bet fails           2   5   10      [Name]
     Trigger: Key technology doesn't mature as expected
     Impact: Programme redesign, delay, cost overrun
```

## Presentation Slide Deck Structure

When presenting product plan to board or investors:

```
Slide 1: Title
Slide 2: Executive Summary (one page from above)
Slide 3: Market Context — trends, competitive landscape
Slide 4: Current Portfolio — strengths, gaps, lifecycle position
Slide 5: Strategic Priorities — what we must achieve
Slide 6: 5-Year Visual Roadmap — timeline view
Slide 7-N: Key Programme Deep-dives — one per major programme
Slide N+1: Investment Profile — summary and cash flow
Slide N+2: Key Risks — top 5 with mitigations
Slide N+3: Decision Points — what approval is needed
Slide N+4: Appendix — detailed schedules, assumptions
```
