# IATF 16949 Audit Questions by Clause

## Quick Reference Guide for Internal Auditors

This reference provides sample audit questions organized by IATF 16949:2016 clause. Use these to guide audit preparation and ensure comprehensive coverage.

---

## Clause 4: Context of the Organization

### 4.1 Understanding the Organization and Its Context

| Question | Look For |
|----------|----------|
| What external issues affect the QMS? | Documented analysis, economic, regulatory, market factors |
| What internal issues affect the QMS? | Culture, technology, knowledge, performance |
| How often are context issues reviewed? | Management review inputs, regular updates |

### 4.2 Understanding Needs and Expectations of Interested Parties

| Question | Look For |
|----------|----------|
| Who are the interested parties? | Customers, employees, suppliers, regulators, community |
| What are their requirements? | Documented requirements list |
| How are changes in requirements monitored? | Review process, update mechanism |

### 4.3 Determining the Scope of the QMS

| Question | Look For |
|----------|----------|
| What is included in the QMS scope? | Documented scope statement |
| Are any requirements excluded? | Justified exclusions (clause 7.1.5.1 only) |
| Does scope include remote sites? | All locations covered |

### 4.4 QMS and Its Processes

| Question | Look For |
|----------|----------|
| How are processes defined? | Process maps, turtle diagrams |
| What are process inputs and outputs? | Documented for each process |
| How do processes interact? | Interaction matrix or flow |
| What are process risks and opportunities? | Risk assessment per process |
