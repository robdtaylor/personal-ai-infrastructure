# Safety Audit Workflow

**Structured workflow for conducting workplace safety audits and inspections.**

## Trigger
- "Safety audit for [area]"
- "Workplace inspection"
- "H&S compliance check"

## Process

### Step 1: Plan the Audit

Define audit scope:

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Audit ID | SA-[YEAR]-[SEQ] |
| Audit Type | Routine / Triggered / External |
| Area/Department | [Where] |
| Date | [Date] |
| Lead Auditor | [Name] |
| Team Members | [Names] |
| Duration | [Hours] |

**Audit Types:**

| Type | Frequency | Scope |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Department inspection | Weekly | Area-specific hazards |
| Safety committee | Monthly | Rotating areas |
| Management review | Quarterly | Programme effectiveness |
| Comprehensive | Annual | Full facility |
| Triggered | As needed | Response to incident/concern |

### Step 2: Prepare for Audit

**Gather Documentation:**
- [ ] Previous audit reports
- [ ] Risk assessments for area
- [ ] COSHH assessments
- [ ] Incident reports (recent)
- [ ] Training records
- [ ] Equipment inspection records
- [ ] Relevant procedures

**Audit Checklist Selection:**
- [ ] General workplace
- [ ] Machine safety
- [ ] Chemical safety
- [ ] Fire safety
- [ ] Emergency equipment
- [ ] PPE compliance
- [ ] Housekeeping/5S

### Step 3: Conduct the Audit

**Opening Meeting (if formal audit):**
- Introduce audit team
- Confirm scope and schedule
- Request area escort
- Explain process

**Audit Approach:**
1. Observe work activities
2. Inspect physical conditions
3. Review documentation
4. Interview employees
5. Check compliance with standards

### Step 4: Employee Interviews

**Sample Questions:**
- Do you know the hazards in your area?
- Have you received H&S training?
- Do you know the emergency procedures?
- Where is the nearest first aid kit/fire extinguisher?
- How do you report hazards or near misses?
- Do you have any safety concerns?

### Step 5: Document Findings

**Finding Classification:**

| Category | Definition | Response |
|----------|------------|----------|
| **Critical** | Immediate danger to life/health | Stop work, fix immediately |
| **Major** | Significant non-compliance | Fix within 7 days |
| **Minor** | Low-risk non-compliance | Fix within 30 days |
| **Observation** | Improvement opportunity | Consider for improvement |
| **Good Practice** | Positive finding | Recognise and share |

**Finding Record:**

```markdown
## Audit Finding

**Finding #:** [SA-XXXX-F01]
**Category:** Critical / Major / Minor / Observation
**Location:** [Specific location]
**Standard:** [Regulation/procedure reference]

**Description:**
[What was found]

**Risk:**
[Potential consequence]

**Recommended Action:**
[What needs to be done]

**Photo Reference:** [If applicable]
```

### Step 6: Closing Meeting

**Discuss with Area Management:**
- Summarise findings
- Highlight critical/major issues
- Agree action owners and dates
- Confirm follow-up process
- Thank participants
